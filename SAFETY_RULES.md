# SAFETY RULES

## Core Safety Rules
- No auto-send.
- No spam.
- No unsolicited WhatsApp.
- No private-data scraping.
- No hidden personal data harvesting.
- No buyer message scraping.
- No auto-reading WhatsApp.
- No auto-reply to buyer.
- No automatic browser opening for buyer contact.
- No quote before stock confirmation.
- No quote before compatibility confirmation.
- No price in quote before final quote eligibility.
- Manual review before buyer reply.
- Manual admin action before sent confirmation.
- Manual buyer reply observation only.
- Manual follow-up planning only.
- Manual deal outcome recording only.
- Manual stock movement review only.
- Manual accounting review only.
- Manual final business review only.

## Stock And Inventory Safety
- Do not update inventory automatically.
- Do not reduce stock automatically.
- Do not reserve stock automatically.
- Do not release stock automatically.
- Do not create stock ledger automatically.
- Manual inventory update is required after review.
- Manual stock ledger entry is required after review.

## Accounting Safety
- Do not create accounting entries automatically.
- Do not create financial ledger entries automatically.
- Do not verify payment automatically.
- Do not collect payment automatically.
- Do not generate receipts automatically.
- Do not create invoices automatically.
- Do not record revenue automatically.
- Manual accounting entry is required after review.

## Final Business Safety
- Do not create final business records automatically.
- Do not close sales automatically.
- Do not move pipeline automatically.
- Do not mark sale won automatically.
- Do not mark lead closed automatically.
- Manual final business record is required after review.
- Manual manager review is required before final close.

## Admin Hub Safety
- Admin Hub is navigation and visibility only.
- Metrics API is read-only.
- Dashboard pages are read-only unless a route is explicitly designed as a manual admin record route.
