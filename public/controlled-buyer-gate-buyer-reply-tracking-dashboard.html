Buyer Reply Tracking Safety Rule

This dashboard is read-only. It displays buyer replies that admin manually observed outside the system. The system does not read WhatsApp, does not scrape buyer messages, does not auto-reply, does not auto-follow-up, does not update inventory, does not create accounting entries, does not close sales, and does not move pipeline.
