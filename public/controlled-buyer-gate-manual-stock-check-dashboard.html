Manual Stock Check Safety Rule

This dashboard is read-only. It displays manual stock status only. Manual stock check does not contact the buyer, send WhatsApp, read WhatsApp, scrape messages, prepare quotes, update inventory, reserve stock, reduce stock, create accounting entries, close sales, or move pipeline.
